Asteroids

GET READY!

Health: 100

Score: 0

How to play: (Zoom out if you can't see all the instructions)

W - Accelarate/Forward (You accelarate for a bit, then move a constant speed. Releasing leads to decelleration)
A - Left
D - Right
SPACE - Shoot
Avoid the asteroids (when shot, they split in two and you get 100 points.
You lose your health while an asteroid is hitting you
Bullets not wrapping is a stylistic choice (The asteroids and player ship will still wrap)
Every second round is a Space Invaders round.
